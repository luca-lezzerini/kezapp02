Appears In: Expiration management
Expiration periods can be either recurring or not.<br/>Recurring will generate a new deadline after an expiration event log recording, non recurring will not.<br/>Example:<br/><ul>
<li>recurring event is yearly check of truck: if a truck has a check, an expiration event log will be recorded and a new deadline (one year after) will be planned.</li><li>non recurring event is the due date of a payment: after payment is done (and recorded in event log) no more payments have to be done and no new deadline is created</li></ul><p/>
