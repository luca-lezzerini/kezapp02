Appears In: Expiration management
A log of all events that have resolved a deadline shall be managed .<br/>Example:<br/><ul>
<li>if a person has attended three training sessions, in the expiration event log shall be recorded all three events and, at each event, the new deadline shall be computed. If training is every year and first training happens at 1 March 2015, the next training deadline will be computed at 1 March 2016. The second training happens at 25 April 2016, next deadline will be 25 April 2017. Last training happens (in late) on 10 June 2017, next deadline will be 10 June 2017.</li></ul>
<br/>An expiration event log con resolve more than one due dates. For example: a course can be attended by many people and can solve both deadlines for safety and for firefighting.<br/>
