This is a calendar for a given resource or resource category.<br/>For example:<br/>All the classrooms have the "classroom calendars"<br/>Meeting room A and J have their own calendar "Meeting Room"<br/>Meeting rooms B, C , D have, again, "classroom calendar"<br/>All meeting rooms have "Office calendar" (for those with their own, this will prevail, as explained later).<br/><br/>If a resource has its own calendar and the related category has another, the resource calendar will prevail in case of conflict.<br/>
